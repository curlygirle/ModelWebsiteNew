about

erin pfeiffer harper

I am a full-time professional model for the agency Boss Models JHB. I started my modelling career in the year 2022. Ever since I can remember, I've been fascinated by the world of fashion and modelling. With hard work and determination, I've been fortunate to turn my passion into a successful career. As a model, I have had the pleasure of working with renowned photographers, designers, and brands, and have gained valuable experience in various aspects of the modelling industry, including runway shows, editorial shoots, and commercial campaigns


With modelling aside, I am a full time third year student at the University of Witwatersrand majoring in Game Design and Interactive Media by sharpening my skills in various aspects of the field. I'm constantly learning and pushing the boundaries of my craft, whether it's designing captivating game levels, crafting user-friendly interfaces, or coding responsive websites. I'm driven by the need to create experiences that are not just physically spectacular, but also meaningful and engaging. I think that design has the ability to alter and improve how people engage.
